Add to Apple Wallet

Get the Perspective Studio digital business card in your Wallet

How to add:

  1. Tap the "Add to Wallet" button above
  2. Your iPhone will open the Wallet app
  3. Tap "Add" in the top right corner
  4. The card will appear in your Wallet!

Important: Open this page in Safari on your iPhone, not in the Files app. The Files app won't trigger the Wallet prompt.

Or visit directly: perspective-card.pkpass
